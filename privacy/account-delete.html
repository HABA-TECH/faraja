Requesting Account Deletion

We value your privacy and offer a straightforward process to request the deletion of your account and associated data. Follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to with the subject line "Account Deletion Request".
  2. Confirm your identity by providing the following information:
    • Full Name
    • Email Address
    • Phone Number
    • National ID Number

Please note that it may take some time to complete the deletion process, and certain data may be retained for a specified period as described below.
